About

FEVERISH - Fact Extraction and VERification with Intelligent Search Heuristics.

Instructions

Enter a factual claim to check in the form above and click "Submit". FEVERISH will retrieve and verify evidence for the claim.

Purpose

FEVERISH is a tool that uses natural language processing to automatically retrieve and verify evidence for a given claim. It is designed to assist fact-checkers and journalists in verifying claims made in news articles and other sources. FEVERISH automates the information retrieval process in the fact-checking pipeline, making it faster and more efficient.

Dataset

FEVERISH uses the FEVER dataset (Fact Extraction and VERification) as its source of information. The FEVER dataset contains over 5 million introductory sections of Wikipedia pages, which are used as evidence to verify claims.

Algorithm
